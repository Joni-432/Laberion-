About Us

Who we are

The Company was founded in 1996 and is the first juice producer in Kosovo who used the Tetra Pak packaging technology. Currently Laberion produces 4 different Juices, Nectars and Still Drinks brands – Eko Frut, Dona, EKS and Punch. In addition to our own brands, we are offering co-packing services to a variety of our clients across South Eastern Europe. Co-packing arrangements can be made with your own brand, your company juice recipe or our technologists can prepare a recipe based on your needs Quality of the products, State of the Art Automated equipment in the factory, qualified staff, trust gained from the work we have done so far with buyers and retailers and the positive attitude of our company guarantee consistent and long term success.
